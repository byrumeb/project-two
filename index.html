Change in the Games


The Olympic Games are the epitome of sports. Just like everything else in the world, they are also being impacted by COVID-19. With health concerns rising, the Games have been rescheduled to the Summer of 2021. Explore here to find out how the change in the Games has been impacting some of the World's best athletes.
